How to Unlock the Vic Viper Charm

(Tested and confirmed to be working on PC as of September 23, 2025.)

To unlock the Vic Viper charm in Dead by Daylight, you need to enter the Konami code with specific prerequisites:

  1. Have Pyramid Head or Cheryl from the Silent Hill chapter unlocked
  2. Equip either Pyramid Head or Cheryl as your character
  3. Return to the main menu (the character must be visible in the lobby)
  4. Enter the Konami code: Up (W), Up (W), Down (S), Down (S), Left (A), Right (D), Left (A), Right (D), Right Mouse Button, Left Mouse Button, Enter
  5. If entered correctly, you'll hear unique DBD lobby music
  6. The Vic Viper charm will now be available in your cosmetics

Console Controls:

  • PlayStation: Up, Up, Down, Down, Left, Right, Left, Right, Circle, Cross, Options
  • Xbox: Up, Up, Down, Down, Left, Right, Left, Right, B, A, Start
  • Nintendo Switch: Up, Up, Down, Down, Left, Right, Left, Right, B, A, Plus

Note: If you can't find the charm after entering the code, try re-entering it quicker or restarting your game.

How to redeem

Redeeming codes in Dead by Daylight is simple. Follow these steps:

  1. Launch Dead by Daylight on your platform (PC, Console)
  2. Navigate to the Store section from the main menu
  3. Select the "Redeem Code" option
  4. Enter the code exactly as shown (they're case-sensitive!)
  5. Confirm and enjoy your rewards!

Note: Some codes may expire quickly or be region locked. We try to note any restrictions in the code description.
